# Content Marketing Strategy: The Integrated Data Engineer

This strategy is designed to leverage your unique, rigorous roadmap to build a powerful personal brand, attract recruiters, and demonstrate your commitment and technical depth. The goal is to turn your 143-week study plan into a compelling narrative of transformation.

---

## 1. Core Narrative & Value Proposition

Your narrative is not "I am learning Data Engineering." It is:

> **"I am building a Data Engineer with a world-class Computer Science foundation. I am tackling the most rigorous curriculum (SICP, MIT 6.824, CS:APP) while simultaneously building production-ready pipelines with modern tools (Airflow, Spark, dbt)."**

This narrative immediately sets you apart from the majority of candidates who only focus on tools.

---

## 2. Platform Strategy & Weekly Cadence

The strategy uses a **Hub-and-Spoke Model**, where **GitHub** and **Articles** are the "Hubs" (deep, permanent content) and **X** and **TikTok** are the "Spokes" (short, engaging, high-reach content).

| Platform | Content Type | Goal | Weekly Cadence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **GitHub** | **Code & Documentation** | Permanent Portfolio, Proof of Work | **1-2 Commits** (Daily/Weekly) |
| **Article (Medium/LinkedIn)** | **Deep Dive/Reflection** | Establish Thought Leadership, SEO | **1 Article** (Every 2-4 Weeks) |
| **X (Twitter)** | **Text/Image Snippets** | Daily Engagement, Micro-Learning | **3-5 Posts** (Daily/Weekly) |
| **TikTok/Shorts** | **Visual/Narrative Video** | High-Reach, Relatability, Personality | **1 Video** (Weekly) |

---

## 3. Weekly Content Template (The "Study Log")

Every week, you will generate content based on the following template, ensuring all platforms are fed from the same source material.

### A. GitHub (The Hub: Proof of Work)

*   **Action:** Create a dedicated repository named `Integrated-DE-Roadmap` or similar.
*   **Structure:**
    *   `README.md`: The full 143-week roadmap.
    *   `/week_XX/`: Folder for each week.
    *   `/week_XX/tycs_notes.md`: Key takeaways from the 3-hour block.
    *   `/week_XX/de_project_code/`: Code for the 2-hour block project/practice.
*   **Weekly Commit:** Commit the code and update the `tycs_notes.md` with the week's key learning.

### B. TikTok/Shorts (The Visual Hook: 30-60 Seconds)

*   **Goal:** Show the *struggle, the win, and the result*.
*   **Format:** **"Day in the Life"** or **"Weekly Recap"** style.
*   **Script Template (Example: Week 1 - SICP & Python):**
    1.  **(Hook - 5s):** "I'm trying to learn the hardest CS book ever, SICP, while building a data pipeline. Here's how Week 1 went." (Show the SICP book/code).
    2.  **(The Struggle - 15s):** Quick cuts of you staring at a complex Scheme function (TYCS Block). Voiceover: "Recursion is breaking my brain, but I spent 15 hours this week on it."
    3.  **(The Win - 15s):** Show the working Python code (DE Block). Voiceover: "But then I used that same recursion logic to build a simple Python function to clean my data. It actually works!"
    4.  **(Call to Action - 5s):** "Follow my 143-week journey. Full code and notes on my GitHub (link in bio)."
*   **Key:** Keep it fast, relatable, and visually show the code/tools.

## 4. Final Advice for Execution

1.  **Consistency is Key:** The most impressive part of this strategy is the **143-week commitment**. Do not miss a weekly TikTok/Shorts video or a Thursday progress post on X. This consistency is what builds trust and a following.
2.  **Be Authentic:** Show the struggle. The most engaging content is not "I'm a genius," but "This was hard, and here is how I figured it out."
3.  **Link Everything:** Every post on X and TikTok must drive traffic to your **GitHub** (the portfolio) and your **Articles** (the thought leadership).
4.  **Timebox Content Creation:** Dedicate **1 hour on Thursday** (your non-study day) to create all content for the week. Do not let content creation eat into your 5 hours of study.

This marketing strategy will transform your personal learning journey into a public, professional portfolio that will impress any recruiter.
