# Algorithms & Data Structures: ADM & Skiena Lectures - Detailed Task Breakdown (180 Blocks)

This document will outline 180 specific study blocks for "Algorithms & Data Structures," based on "The Algorithm Design Manual" (ADM) by Steven Skiena and his accompanying lectures. Each block is designed for approximately 50 minutes of focused study.

**Resource Key:**
*   **ADM:** *The Algorithm Design Manual, 3rd Edition* (Steven S. Skiena) - PDF or physical copy.
*   **Skiena Lect:** Steven Skiena's CSE373 Analysis of Algorithms Lectures (e.g., Fall 2021 or similar recent playlist from www3.cs.stonybrook.edu/~skiena/373/videos/ or YouTube).
*   **Ex:** Exercises from ADM (end-of-chapter problems or specific examples).
*   **WS:** War Stories from ADM (to be read for context and motivation).

---

**Part I: Practical Algorithm Design (ADM Chapters 1-12)**

**Module 1: Introduction to Algorithm Design (ADM Chapter 1) (Approx. 10 Blocks)**

*   **Block 1 (Task 1/180): What is an Algorithm? Correctness & Efficiency**
    *   ADM: Chapter 1.1 (What is an Algorithm?), 1.2 (Robot Tour Optimization - WS)
    *   Skiena Lect 1: Introduction, What is an Algorithm? (first ~30-40 min)
*   **Block 2 (Task 2/180): Demonstrating Correctness**
    *   ADM: Chapter 1.3 (Selecting the Right Jobs - WS), 1.4 (Reasoning About Correctness - Induction, Loop Invariants)
    *   Skiena Lect 1: Remainder (Correctness, Examples)
*   **Block 3 (Task 3/180): Induction Examples & Problem Solving**

    *   Ex: ADM 1.4, 1.5
*   **Block 6 (Task 6/180): Efficiency & Asymptotic Notation (Big O)**
    *   ADM: Chapter 1.6 (About the War Stories - WS), Chapter 2.1 (The RAM Model of Computation), 2.2 (The Big Oh Notation)

    *   Ex: ADM 2.1, 2.2, 2.3
*   **Block 8 (Task 8/180): Analyzing Algorithm Runtimes - Examples**
    *   ADM: Chapter 2.3 (Growth Rates and Dominance Relations), 2.4 (Working with the Big Oh - examples)
    *   Skiena Lect 3: Algorithm Analysis Examples (first ~30 min)
*   **Block 9 (Task 9/180): Reasoning About Efficiency - Best, Worst, Average Case**
    *   ADM: Chapter 2.5 (Reasoning About Efficiency - Best, Worst, Average Case)
    *   Ex: ADM 2.4, 2.5, 2.6, 2.7
